Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

Proofcam (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

Information Collection

Proofcam does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, photos, videos, or user-generated content on external servers.

All captured photos and videos remain stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them manually.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera — to capture photos and videos
  • Microphone — to record audio during video recording
  • Storage / Photos — to save photos and videos to your device gallery

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the App.

Advertising

Proofcam may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising providers may automatically collect certain non-personal device information to provide and improve advertisements, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location
  • App interaction and diagnostic information

Proofcam does not directly collect, access, or store this information.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies

Data Storage

All photos and videos created using the App are stored locally on your device storage.

The App does not upload, back up, or synchronize your media files to any external server or cloud platform.

Children’s Privacy

Proofcam is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
